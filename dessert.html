Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Whoopie Pies

Recipe:

For the Cookies:

2 cups brown sugar, 1 cup vegetable oil, 2 eggs, 1.5 cups canned pumpkin, 3 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soad, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice, 1 Tbsp cinnamon, 1 cup cocolate chips

Mix the brown sugar and oil

Add eggs and pumpkin, and mix

Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, pumpkin pie spice, and cinnamon, and mix.

Stir in chocolate chips and pipe onto baking sheet

Bake at 350 for 10 minutes

For the Filling:

8 oz soften cream cheese, 1/2 cup softened butter, 1 cup powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla

Beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth.

Mix in powdered sugar and vanilla.

Spread onto half of the cooled cookies, and top with the rest of the cookies.
