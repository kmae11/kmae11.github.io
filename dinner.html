One Pot Chicken Alfredo

Recipe:

olive oil, chicken, garlic, chicken broth, heavy cream, penne, parmesan

In a pot heat the oil and brown the chicken.

Add garlic, and saute

Add broth, cream and pasta an bring to a boil

Cover and reduce heat to a simmer for 15-20 minutes.

Turn off heat and stir in parmesan cheese

Review:

Super easy dish to make. Perfect for cooking dinner in a time crunch. Also really easy to adjust depending on how many people you are cooking for. I struggled knowing how long to cook the chicken for. Maybe thats because I'm used to preparing dinner in a microwave?! I could definitelly see myself making this dish again.
